What is meWe

meWE is a website to increase mental illness awareness. The first step of being aware is to know more about mental illness. Thus, meWE also introduces some types of mental illnesses, what kind of treatment there is, and how to be a supportive caregiver.

me – stands for mental illness
WE – stands for ME and YOU

meWE wants to bring out a message that is everyone should concern about mental health and mental illness because it is not someone else’s business, but me and you! Diseases have no discriminations, it can attack anyone of this world, either physical illness or mental illness, and regardless of your age, gender, and race. Now it’s time to eliminate the stigmas and gives real acceptance to people with mental illness.
