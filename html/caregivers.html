Caregiver

When someone around you is diagnosed with a mental health disorder, you can be a caregiver who helps and supports him/her with love and understanding. The caregiver plays a crucial role, which makes those with the condition feel they are not alone. We need to realize that the situations of mental illness do not only affect individuals but also their family members, friends, neighbors, coworkers, and others in the community.
The very first step of being a caregiver is you need to know about some knowledge regarding mental health and mental illness. When you become more knowledgeable, then you can only be a caregiver who gives the right and suitable support.

They Are Asking For Help!

Taking care of family member

Mental health condition does not only affect an individual, and also their family members. Support and accompanying family members can make a huge difference. Although you might face a lot of problems, remember you are not alone and also in the process of learning.
It is hard to control every situation that might arise, and there must be some situations you will feel difficult or even want to give up. Remember, you are still learning.
